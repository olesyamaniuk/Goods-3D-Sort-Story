Game Description

Goods 3D Sort Story is a delightful and relaxing 3D sorting game where you organize cute items into the right boxes. From toys to daily essentials, your mission is to declutter the chaos! Swipe, tap, and drop goods into their places to complete satisfying levels. With stunning visuals and stress-free gameplay, it's the perfect game to unwind with!

FAQ

  • Is the game free to play?
    Yes! Goods 3D Sort Story is free to download and play. Optional in-app purchases are available for cosmetic items and boosters.
  • Can I play without an internet connection?
    Absolutely. The game supports offline play, so you can enjoy sorting even without Wi-Fi or mobile data.
  • How many levels are there?
    New levels are added regularly! Currently, there are hundreds of levels designed to keep you engaged and entertained.
  • Are there ads in the game?
    There are optional ads that you can watch to earn extra rewards, but gameplay won’t be interrupted by mandatory ads.
  • I’m stuck on a level, what can I do?
    You can use boosters or undo moves to help you through tricky levels. Keep an eye out for helpful tips during gameplay too!
